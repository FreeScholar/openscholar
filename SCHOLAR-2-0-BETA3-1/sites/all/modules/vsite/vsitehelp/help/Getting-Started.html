Assuming you have a valid email address, you can set up your web site simply by going to the site registration page and entering a user name, your email address, and your preferred domain name.

You will be sent an email welcoming you to your new site with a one-time temporary password. Click on the link provided in the email (or copy and paste the link into your browser) and click the log in button.
