'Users' refers to others who you've selected to either freely comment on your posts or – in the case of faculty assistants – to post material for you. In either case you must initiate an invitation to an individual to become a site member.

To do so click on the Users menu link in the Control Panel and then to Members. To add a new user click on the "Add new user" link in red and fill out the form. The individual will be notified that you've requested that they be a site member/user.

Admin Privileges

You can grant admin privileges for assistants once the user has logged in and has an account. Simply return to the Members admin area and click "Admin: Create" for that person. They can now create content on your site.
