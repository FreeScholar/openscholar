Content can be categorized both both feature-specific categorizes, or by site-wide categories. Categorizing your content can be quite useful to both you and the users of your site. For instance you may have a blog entry, a review of a new book on Camus, and you may choose to categorize this blog post as "Camus", "absurdism", and "literary crititism". Meanwhile you may also haved posted some class material that focused on literary criticism on a different topic, which you categorize as "literary criticism". Both the class material and your blog post on Camus can now appear together if a user searches your site for "literary criticism" or clicks on the category links that will appear in your posts.

Adding Categories

You can add new categories by going to the Control Panel, clicking the "Content" menu button and then "Taxonomy", and the "Add category" link
